Find My iPhone From PC Windows, MAC, iCloud

Well, Being an iPhone User The worst nightmare is if your phone gets stolen or you lost it somewhere accidentally. Although Nobody gonna use your data as apple has strong security but still its a worried scenario. In this case, make use of find my iPhone from PC.

Actually Apple has this feature in icloud.com initially where users can find their iPhone’s location. if you find suddenly that your iPhone is missing. In spite of taking all safety measures and being alert in handling your iPhone, it might happen, that you have kept your phone somewhere and now you are not able to locate it. In this situation, you will be required to take help of this feature from the iPhone. You can access this feature, find my iPhone from Mac, Windows or PC. It really works, and you will easily locate the exact place where your iPhone is at the time.

How Find my iPhone Works?

This amazing and super helpful feature from iPhone works by using the Location Services that are enabled in your iPhone. Location services include your phone’s GPS, cell towers and Wi-Fi. Basically, it is all the magic of location services. These services allow Apple and other third-party applications or website, to gather and use the information based on the current location of your iPhone, thus helping in finding your lost iPhone.

Find My iPhone From Windows PC Computer

Indeed, it is absolutely correct to say that Apple devices are really easy to handle and use. They are so sorted and simple. In case, you are not finding your iPhone and you need to access this feature of iPhone. You just have to follow a few straight and easy steps, here you go!

  • Browse, icloud.com from any desired browser.
  • Log in with your Apple ID and password.
  • All devices will appear on a map, just tap to All Devices.
  • Locate, find my iPhone and tap.
  • Tap to put a sound to your lost device.
  • Put your device in Lost Mode or erase your device.
  • You are done!

The above-mentioned steps will help you locate your Apple devices (iPhone, iPad, iPod, Mac and Apple Watch, also), as long as it is set up correctly and without error.

Find my iPhone is not working solution

if you face another headbanging situation and you are not able to use the find my iPhone feature on your device then what? We have a solution to this also. Just have patience, and follow a few other steps!

Check whether find my iPhone, is enabled or not!

  • Go to Settings > iCloud> find my iPhone.
  • Enable the switch next to Find my iPhone.
  • Turn on the, Send Lost Location.

If to explain in detail, all you need to do is, go to the settings and then iCloud and there, enable the Find my iPhone setting. Next, you will be required to see whether the switch next to Find my iPhoneis enabled or not. In case it is not, then tap it and it will turn green, notifying you that it is on. Always keep in mind that you have also enabled the Send Lost Location service while performing this step, doing this will help to get the location of your iPhone, iPad or iPod, even if your computer or the other device is running out of power.

Turn on the Find My iPhone in your Location Services.

  • Go to Settings > Privacy > Location Services.
  • Tap on Find My iPhone.

If Find My iPhone is enabled in your iPhone and it is showing online, still you are not able to access this feature, check out your location services tab. If it is not enabled, follow the above mentioned two steps, and scroll down do locate Find My iPhone, tap on that and your service will be enabled.

Find My iPhone Through iCloud

All the earlier mentioned steps will work only and only if your iPhone is online. If in any case, the iPhone is not active (online) then none of these steps would work accurately. In this case, you will be required to perform steps through iCloud.com. Now, the question is, how will you get notified that your iPhone is in online mode or offline mode? So, basically, when you will try the above-mentioned steps, the iCloud website will itself get a grey dot next to your iPhone’s last known location. You can easily set up the program so that it tells you the next time when your iPhone is online. Follow the following easy steps:

  • Click on the All Devices, in the drop-down menu.
  • Choose your iPhone and tap on it.
  • A box will appear in the upper right corner of the web browser.
  • Tap, to set the program as Notify Me When Found.
  • Again, tap to the Sound icon.
  • Enable the alarm, to notify you when iPhone is online.

If even after all this hassle, you are not able to locate your iPhone, and you have discovered it is lost or stolen, then you can simply put your phone on Lost Mode. And through this same window just Erase your iPhone. Just choose, ERASE iPhone.
