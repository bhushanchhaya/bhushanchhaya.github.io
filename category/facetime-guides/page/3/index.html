Step by Step Guide to Change Apple Id Email

In case, you have got a new email id and you do not want to make use of your former id, then it is mandatory to change your Apple ID. This task needs to be performed keeping mind the safety measures of your account.

Changing your Apple ID is not at all complicated, rather it is really easy and simple if you are following each and every step suggested by the official site. To help you in this matter, today’s article is about the step by step guide to change Apple email id.

how to change apple id email

Your Apple ID is really important if you want to access any of your Apple Cloud services. These cloud services include – iTunes, Apple Music and iCloud. Generally, the ID is generated as @mac or @cloud. But in case you are also using any third-party email address like @gmail.com, then you must keep it to one.

This means you need to change your email address and id for safety purposes. It includes the email address and password that you must use for signing in and contacts.

How to Change?

Before moving any further, we must get one thing very clear. The Apple ID is basically an email address just like some third-party email address like [any person]@gmail.com or [any person]@yahoo.com, but the passwords for both the email addresses cannot be same.

Until and unless, you have mentioned the same password for both the email addresses, which you should strictly not do, but generally people commit this blunder!

Make it a point, that you are very well aware of your password before proceeding to any changes because everywhere you will be asked the password as the step of verification.

If you know your current Apple ID and a correct password for it, you can easily change your email id from any browser. Now let’s begin!

  • From a web browser on your iMac, iPad, iPhone or computers, just navigate to appleid.apple.com
  • Enter the Apple Id and your password.
  • Now, log in the id by tapping the arrow sign.
  • You will be now required to verify your id with the two-factor authentication (if enabled).
  • In the account section, click EDIT next to “REACHABLE AT”.
  • Click onto, change Apple email id under the Apple Id.
  • Enter the new e-mail address.
  • Click on continue.
  • You will get a verification code on the new email id.
  • Open the email from your Apple.
  • Click on the verify email id link in the email you received.
  • Enter the verification code at appleid.apple.com
  • Click on verify.
  • Click on done, once finished with the process.

Check the following GIF if you wants to have video tutorial.

These are the only steps that you will be required to perform in order to change Apple mail id. But in case you are not fortunate enough, and your email address is already being used with an Apple Id, then you will need to walk some extra miles, to get the work done. You will be required to make use of three different email accounts for this purpose.

  • The email account associated with the current Apple ID.
  • The email account associated with the Apple Id you want to change your current Apple Id to.
  • The third email account is that account that can be used throw-away email (any account that you have created earlier, and you know its password)

Now the steps required to solve the problem are: –

  1. You will be needed to get the password for the Apple ID associated with the email that you want to change to.
  • From the web browser, navigate to iforgot.apple.com.
  • Enter the email address you want to switch your Apple account to.
  • Click on continue.
  • Select I need to reset my password.
  • Click on continue.
  • Select get an email.
  • Click on continue again.
  • Open the email you receive from Apple.
  • Click reset now.
  • Enter a new password.
  • Re-enter the new password.
  • Click on Reset password.

Change the email associated with the inactive Apple ID

  • With the above-mentioned sub-steps of step one, now you have access to your inactive Apple ID.
  • Follow the steps for changing the email associated with your Apple ID.
  • Use the Apple Id password and email address from the first step mentioned above.
  • Change the email associated with that account to the throw-away email address.

Go back and change your active Apple email ID

  • Now, that the email address you want to make use of for your Apple Id is freed, you can make changes.
  • Follow the steps for changing the email address associated with your Apple ID.
  • When you will enter the new email address, you will get the address you want to use.

If your email address ends with @icloud.com, @mac.com or @me.com than you cannot change the Apple ID to any of the third-party email addresses. In such a case, you will also not see the option of Change Apple Id, on your account page or the option to delete your email address from your iPad, iMac or iPhone.

In case you are highly unfortunate, and none of the solutions works for you then the only possible thing to save you is, download as much content as possible in your devices or any other cloud-based services (like photos, media, contacts, iCloud Drive Documents, contents that are not purchased from iTunes etc.) , and start over from scratch, with your preferred email address account.

FINAL WORDS

We have discussed, what is the need to change Apple email ID, and the steps to do the same. We all have so much on our phones and emails. In fact, we manage most of our payments and money transfers from our devices. A lot of other important stuff is transferred over the email which is crucial to keep safe. To ensure efficient privacy and security, it is highly recommended to keep yourself alert and aware of all technicalities involved in the process. All your passwords need to be protected so that your account is safe from different online frauds and threats.

Find My iPhone From PC Windows, MAC, iCloud

Well, Being an iPhone User The worst nightmare is if your phone gets stolen or you lost it somewhere accidentally. Although Nobody gonna use your data as apple has strong security but still its a worried scenario. In this case, make use of find my iPhone from PC.

Actually Apple has this feature in icloud.com initially where users can find their iPhone’s location. if you find suddenly that your iPhone is missing. In spite of taking all safety measures and being alert in handling your iPhone, it might happen, that you have kept your phone somewhere and now you are not able to locate it. In this situation, you will be required to take help of this feature from the iPhone. You can access this feature, find my iPhone from Mac, Windows or PC. It really works, and you will easily locate the exact place where your iPhone is at the time.

How Find my iPhone Works?

This amazing and super helpful feature from iPhone works by using the Location Services that are enabled in your iPhone. Location services include your phone’s GPS, cell towers and Wi-Fi. Basically, it is all the magic of location services. These services allow Apple and other third-party applications or website, to gather and use the information based on the current location of your iPhone, thus helping in finding your lost iPhone.

Find My iPhone From Windows PC Computer

Indeed, it is absolutely correct to say that Apple devices are really easy to handle and use. They are so sorted and simple. In case, you are not finding your iPhone and you need to access this feature of iPhone. You just have to follow a few straight and easy steps, here you go!

  • Browse, icloud.com from any desired browser.
  • Log in with your Apple ID and password.
  • All devices will appear on a map, just tap to All Devices.
  • Locate, find my iPhone and tap.
  • Tap to put a sound to your lost device.
  • Put your device in Lost Mode or erase your device.
  • You are done!

The above-mentioned steps will help you locate your Apple devices (iPhone, iPad, iPod, Mac and Apple Watch, also), as long as it is set up correctly and without error.

Find my iPhone is not working solution

if you face another headbanging situation and you are not able to use the find my iPhone feature on your device then what? We have a solution to this also. Just have patience, and follow a few other steps!

Check whether find my iPhone, is enabled or not!

  • Go to Settings > iCloud> find my iPhone.
  • Enable the switch next to Find my iPhone.
  • Turn on the, Send Lost Location.

If to explain in detail, all you need to do is, go to the settings and then iCloud and there, enable the Find my iPhone setting. Next, you will be required to see whether the switch next to Find my iPhoneis enabled or not. In case it is not, then tap it and it will turn green, notifying you that it is on. Always keep in mind that you have also enabled the Send Lost Location service while performing this step, doing this will help to get the location of your iPhone, iPad or iPod, even if your computer or the other device is running out of power.

Turn on the Find My iPhone in your Location Services.

  • Go to Settings > Privacy > Location Services.
  • Tap on Find My iPhone.

If Find My iPhone is enabled in your iPhone and it is showing online, still you are not able to access this feature, check out your location services tab. If it is not enabled, follow the above mentioned two steps, and scroll down do locate Find My iPhone, tap on that and your service will be enabled.

Find My iPhone Through iCloud

All the earlier mentioned steps will work only and only if your iPhone is online. If in any case, the iPhone is not active (online) then none of these steps would work accurately. In this case, you will be required to perform steps through iCloud.com. Now, the question is, how will you get notified that your iPhone is in online mode or offline mode? So, basically, when you will try the above-mentioned steps, the iCloud website will itself get a grey dot next to your iPhone’s last known location. You can easily set up the program so that it tells you the next time when your iPhone is online. Follow the following easy steps:

  • Click on the All Devices, in the drop-down menu.
  • Choose your iPhone and tap on it.
  • A box will appear in the upper right corner of the web browser.
  • Tap, to set the program as Notify Me When Found.
  • Again, tap to the Sound icon.
  • Enable the alarm, to notify you when iPhone is online.

If even after all this hassle, you are not able to locate your iPhone, and you have discovered it is lost or stolen, then you can simply put your phone on Lost Mode. And through this same window just Erase your iPhone. Just choose, ERASE iPhone.

Last Words

Hope, this article will help you locate your iPhone easily through Find my iPhone application. By following simple security measures, you can easily keep your iPhone safe and secured. With the above-mentioned steps and procedures, you can get the location of your missing iPhone, without any more headache and worries.

Add Songs Without iTunes in iPhone

Limitations when you are using iTunes, to add music to your iPhone:

Down below, we will be learning about different methods to add music to your iPhone without iTunes.

The music manager will show you the locations where your songs are saved.

Select your desired location where you want to scan the file.

If your songs are saved somewhere else, then you are required to click ADD FOLDER and add the folder to scan.

Download the Google Music play application from Google Play on your iPhone.

All your songs will be transferred to your iPhone.

Using Spotify premium

Sign up for the Spotify premium.

Download and install the Spotify premium on your computer.

Click the “FILE” menu in the player, and select “Preferences”.

Click “add sources” to choose the folder you want to transfer to Spotify premium.

Download and install the Spotify application on your iPhone, using Play Store.

Click the “device” menu, and select your iPhone from the list.

Click “sync this device with Spotify”.

Open the Spotify app on your iPhone.

Tap the menu button, and select your music.

Tap the “playlist” option, then select “local files”.

Slide the “available offline” slider on the right top of the screen, to take your music offline.

Through the Dropbox

Sign up for the Dropbox account.

Install the Dropbox in your computer.

Copy the music that you want to add to your iPhone, into this Dropbox folder.

Wait for the music files to upload.

Download the Dropbox app on your iPhone.

Tap onto the song, you want to play.

Mark the songs as the favourite, to get them available offline.

Through the MediaMonkey

Download and install the MediaMonkey.

Download the basic iTunes services that you will be needing.

Download the iTunes Installer from apple.com/iTunes/download.

Rename the iTunesSetup.exe to iTunesSetup.zip.

Double click the zip file to open it, and drag it to your desktop.

Double click the file on your desktop, to install the connection services.

Download and install QuickTime from Windows.

In case, you are using iTunes, open it to adjust some settings. (If you do not use iTunes, skip this step)

Open MediaMonkey while your iPhone is still connected.

Select the device (iPhone) in the left menu.

Tap onto the “Auto-sync” tab.

Click the “Options” tab.

Add your desired music files to MediaMonkey library.

Sync the file to your iPhone.

Using the WhatsApp

Download the app in your phones.

Start a chat with yourself.

Allow the WhatsApp to backup to your camera roll.

Open computers.

Download the music and songs that you want to transfer on your iPhone.
